About My Favorite Candy

little bits of sweet candy that pop in your mouth. You can hear and taste this candy. Whether you're a fan or not, Pop Rocks are most certainly a memorable candy experience. Pop Rocks are a retro candy favourite and they pack quite a punch with their explosive, sweet and fizzy, fruity flavour.
